Saucy Gochujang Noodles with Chicken

Spicy, peanutty, and packed with flavor — these saucy gochujang noodles are your new weeknight favorite!

Author: Lindsay Total Time: 30 minutes Yield: 4–6 servings

Ingredients

For the Gochujang Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2–3 tablespoons gochujang sauce (adjust for spice level)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1–2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1–2 cups broth or water (for thinning)

For the Ramen

  • 1 pound ground chicken (or pork)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 packets ramen or stir-fry noodles (just the noodles)
  • 1–2 cups fresh spinach
  • ¼ cup chopped chives, scallions, cilantro, or basil (for topping)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil (for finishing)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (for finishing)

Instructions

  1. Make the sauce: Whisk all sauce ingredients (except the extra broth) in a bowl or shake together in a jar until smooth and thick.
  2. Cook the chicken: In a large skillet, cook ground chicken over medium-high heat. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Boil the noodles: Cook noodles for a few minutes to soften, then drain and set aside.
  4. Combine everything: Add spinach, noodles, and the sauce to the pan with the chicken. Toss over medium-high heat until the spinach is wilted. Gradually add broth or water (about 1½ cups) until you reach your desired saucy consistency.
  5. Serve: Top with herbs, scallions, chili oil, sesame seeds, or any toppings you love. Serve hot and enjoy the ultimate comfort meal!

Notes

Spice level: The heat depends on the brand of gochujang you use. Bibigo and P.F. Chang’s brands are milder and family-friendly. For more spice, add chili oil or extra gochujang when serving.

Protein swap: Ground pork or turkey works perfectly in place of chicken.

Extra flavor tip: Add a drizzle of sesame oil and a squeeze of lime before serving for a perfect balance of heat and freshness.
