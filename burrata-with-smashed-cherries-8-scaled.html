Burrata with Crushed Cherries and Pistachios

A fresh and delicious appetizer featuring creamy burrata, cherries, and pistachios.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (455 grams) burrata
  • 1 pound (455 grams) fresh cherries
  • Olive oil
  • 1/3 cup (40 grams) salted, shelled pistachios
  • A sprig or two of fresh mint
  • Flaky sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Crostini for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the burrata: Drain the burrata on a paper towel and let it come to room temperature for maximum creaminess. Tear into chunks and place on a serving platter.
  2. Crush the cherries: Use the side of a knife or the bottom of a glass to crush the cherries. Remove the pits and tear the cherries in half. Pour any accumulated cherry juices over the burrata.
  3. Prepare the pistachios: Coarsely chop the pistachios or crush them using the back of a heavy pan.
  4. Assemble: Drizzle the burrata and cherries with olive oil. Sprinkle the pistachios, flaky sea salt, black pepper, and fresh mint on top.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately with crostini and enjoy!
