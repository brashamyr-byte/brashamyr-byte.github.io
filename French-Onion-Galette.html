French Onion Galette

Transform your savory cravings into this golden, caramelized French Onion Galette with Gruyère cheese and mustard.

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp (1 stick) cold unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup + 3 tbsp ice water
  • 3 pounds yellow onions (about 3 large or 6 medium)
  • 10 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter (for filling)
  • 1 tsp kosher salt (for filling)
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper (for filling)
  • 6 oz Gruyère cheese
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tsp balsamic or sherry vinegar
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Make the crust: Freeze butter for 15 minutes, then grate it and mix with flour and pepper. Add ice water and form dough. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare the filling: Slice onions and cook with thyme, salt, and pepper until golden brown, about 45-60 minutes. Add wine and vinegar to deglaze.
  3. Assemble the galette: Preheat oven to 400°F. Roll out the dough on parchment, spread Dijon mustard, sprinkle half the cheese, then layer caramelized onions. Fold dough edges over, sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  4. Bake: Bake for 35 minutes until the crust is golden-brown and cheese is bubbly. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your warm, crispy French Onion Galette. Perfect for a cozy dinner or a delicious appetizer.
