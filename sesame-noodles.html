Shortcut Sesame Butter Noodles

Quick sesame butter noodles using convenient packaged saucy noodles.

Author: Lindsay Total Time: 12 minutes Yield: 1–2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 (3.35 oz) package saucy shortcut noodles (Momofuku Spicy Soy or Soy Scallion recommended)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1–2 teaspoons honey
  • ½ tablespoon toasted sesame oil (more to taste)
  • A splash of water
  • Optional toppings: green onions, sesame seeds, chili crisp, or togarashi

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles: Prepare according to the package instructions, including the provided sauce packet.
  2. Add the extras: Toss the hot noodles gently with butter, honey, and sesame oil. Adjust flavors to your liking. The noodles should be silky when hot — if they get too sticky, add a splash of warm water to loosen them and create a light, creamy texture.
  3. Finish and serve: Top with chili crisp, scallions, sesame seeds, or togarashi. Perfect for a quick snack or serve with a fried egg, salad, ground chicken, or shrimp for a full meal!

Extra Tips

Adjust stickiness: Rinse noodles briefly after boiling for a silkier texture. A short rinse keeps some starch for sauce clinginess. For extra grip, reheat briefly on low heat after mixing with sauce.

Flavor balance: Add a few drops more sesame oil or honey depending on your taste. This recipe is all about adjusting to your perfect level of sweet, nutty, and savory!
